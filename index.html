About

Qraft Academy is a cohort based career development apprenticeships program that helps young people looking for work or pursuing entrepreneurship as a career.

The Academy provides participants with the ability to self-discovery, access to experiential opportunities and meaningful jobs. The outcome is to expose participants to a diverse range of opportunities to build experience and clarity about their goals..

Mission

To provide tailor- made career services to individuals and career developers.
