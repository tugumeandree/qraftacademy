About

Qraft Academy is a cohort based career development apprenticeship program that helps young people looking to kick start careers in digital technology and visual art.

The Academy provides participants with the ability to self-discovery, access to experiential opportunities and meaningful work. The outcome is to expose participants to a diverse range of opportunities to build experience and clarity about their career goals.

Mission

To provide tailor- made career services to young people
